About Alina

Praised for her radiant timbre, refined musicality, and compelling stage presence, soprano Alina Tkachuk is rapidly establishing herself among the most promising young voices on the international opera scene.

In 2025 she makes several important debuts: Adina (L’elisir d’amore) at the Grand Theatre in Łódź, Giulia (La scala di seta) at Teatro Coccia in Novara — where Una Voce Poco Fa hailed her performance as “fresh, lively, and full of charm” — and Lucy (The Telephone) at Teatro Massimo Bellini in Catania. She also appears as Liù (Turandot) at the Puccini Festival in Torre del Lago under Alfonso Signorini and Renato Palumbo, where Opera Online praised her “lyric glow and emotional sincerity”.
